About Hacktoberfest

Hacktoberfest is a month-long celebration of open source software. It encourages people to contribute to open source projects and learn about the world of coding. It's a fantastic opportunity for both beginners and experienced developers to get involved in the open source community.

How to Participate

To participate in Hacktoberfest 2023, follow these simple steps:

  1. Register on the Hacktoberfest website.
  2. Make four pull requests to any public repositories on GitHub.
  3. Contribute and help the open source community.
